Our Courses & Programs

Toddler Cubs (1-2 years)

Our Toddler Cubs program is a vibrant world of sensory exploration. We focus on developing fine motor skills, early language acquisition, and positive social interactions through music, art, and guided play. It's the perfect first step into a learning environment.

Lion Leapers (3-4 years)

The Lion Leapers curriculum builds on foundational skills. Children engage in pre-reading activities, basic math concepts, science experiments, and creative arts. We encourage independence and problem-solving in a structured yet flexible setting.

Pride Leaders (4-5 years)

This pre-kindergarten program is designed to ensure your child is ready for the next big step! We focus on advanced literacy, writing, critical thinking, and collaborative projects. Graduates from this program leave as confident, curious, and capable learners.
